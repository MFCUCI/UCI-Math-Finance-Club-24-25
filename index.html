Frequently Asked Questions

Math Finance Club

What is the Math Finance Club? +
The Math Finance Club is dedicated to helping students prepare for successful careers in finance and related analytical fields. From workshops, guest speakers, professional development, and the Spring Finance-a-thon, the Math Finance Club teaches students practical real work skills that will transfer to the workplace. While we invite all majors, all math, economics, quantitative economics, or computer science majors are especially encouraged to join.
How do I join? +
All Math Finance Club meetings are free to attend. Meetings are posted on our Instagram and Discord. For students interested in more tailored advice, an exclusive membership is available for a small fee. The benefits of exclusive membership are a professional headshot, exclusive socials, professional resumes, quant projects, free MFC merch, priority announcements, connecting with professionals and job application help.
What are meetings times, dates, and locations? +
At the minimum, weekly meetings are held for general members. Location and specific dates and times will be determined quarterly, based on room availability, and will be posted on all our socials accordingly.

Finance-a-thon

Why should I join? +
Joining the Finance-a-thon at UCI is an incredible opportunity to gain hands-on real world experience. You will have the opportunity to work in a team, solve coding and math problems, develop and present a case study, and network with real companies. Plus, free meals are provided all three days!
Who can attend? +
This event is only open to UCI students. Anyone interested in professions in finance, quant finance, mathematics, or related quantitative or analytical professions are especially encouraged to join.
How do I sign up? +
Fill out the Google form for the Finance-a-thon (Link within the Linktree on our Instagram bio).
I don‚Äôt have any experience in finance. Can I still participate? +
Yes! All skill levels are encouraged to participate. The competition is beginner-friendly, so perfect for someone looking to start their finance, tech, or math journey!
When is the event hosted? +
The event is hosted from May 16th, 2025 at 5pm to May 18th, 2025, 1pm. It is not overnight, so please make your own sleeping arrangements.
How much does it cost? +
The Finance-a-thon competition is completely free!
How can I prepare? +
We encourage students to familiarize themselves with Python, as it will be used as primary means in the competition. While no prior experience with Python is required, having at least one person on a team with basic understanding of the coding language beforehand can set teams up for success.
How do teams work? +
Teams will be made of 4 students, and teams will be established before the competition. After signing up, you will be sent a link where you can indicate your preferred teammates, if you have any in mind. Otherwise, we will hold an in-person meeting to find teammates.
